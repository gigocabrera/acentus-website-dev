Hello, we are Acentus, we offer...

Medical supplies for people with special healthcare needs!

Medical Nutrition

Understanding insurance benefit for medical nutrition products can be a challenge. Acentus has over 20 years of insight into insurance coverage of enteral feeding supplies and medical nutrition products

Incontinence

Acentus has years of experience helping clients who use incontinence products. Our team understands the complex coverage criteria and documentation required for insurance coverage of these supplies

Urology

Acentus’s experienced team understands the importance of compliance to a urinary catheter plan of care. Our team understands the complex coverage criteria and documentation required for insurance coverage of these supplies

Wound Care

Acentus's chronic wound program helps reduce healing time by providing customers with access to the latest advancements in dressing technology

Why Choose Acentus?

Since 2003, the Acentus team has partnered with Managed Care Organizations across the country building affordable supply solutions for medically needy members. Through comprehensive training, strong manufacturer relationships and a robust technology platform, we establish aggressive managed care programs designed to fit the needs of today's health care environment. No other company can match Acentus' member satisfaction, distribution footprint or rate schedule
